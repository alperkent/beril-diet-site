General Advice

Essential guidelines for a healthier life.

Daily Habits

  • Water: Do not wait until you are thirsty. Drink 2-2.5 liters daily.
  • Movement: Walk at least 30 minutes a day. Don't stand still on escalators!
  • Mineral Water: 1-2 bottles of natural mineral water daily are great for minerals.
  • Snacks: Don't skip snacks. Skipping them leads to cheating later.
  • Sleep: Stop eating 2 hours before bedtime.
  • Salt: Don't add extra salt to cooked meals.
  • Salads: If your main dish has oil, skip oil in the salad. Use lemon, vinegar, or natural pomegranate molasses directly.
  • Variety: Add steamed broccoli or cauliflower to your salads to boost variety.
  • Mindful Eating: Eat with love and awareness. Enjoy every bite.
  • Weighing: Weigh yourself only once a week in the morning.
