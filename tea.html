Metabolism Tea

A soothing blend to boost digestion and metabolism.

Drink after meals or whenever you want.

üçµ

Ingredients

  • 1 Liter Water
  • 5 Black Peppercorns
  • 5 Cloves
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 1 piece of Ginger
  • (Optional) 2 pinches of Rosemary
  • (Optional) Apple peel or slices

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
  2. Bring to a boil/brew for 5 minutes.
  3. Strain and serve warm.
